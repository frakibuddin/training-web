About us

Who we are

The Ted Professional Training Association is a training institution that operates in the province of Palermo. It has two offices in Palermo (in Corso dei Mille 1608 and via Caduti del Lavori 58) in Carini in Via Giovanni Pascoli 12. The institution also has offices in Belmonte Mezzagno, in via Michelangelo Buonarroti 4, in Misilmeri in Viale Europa 558 and in Castelvetrano in via Campobello 104. The head office is in Monreale in via Baronio Manfredi 27. The institution mainly organizes free VET courses, education and training for young people aged 13 to 17 years. Furthermore, the structure carries out training courses for adults, in line with the programming of the Sicilian Region. Ted Professional Training is also an employment agency and in its offices in Palermo, Monreale, Misilmeri, Corleone and Carini it accompanies the unemployed to work within programs intended for this purpose such as the one called "Youth Guarantee"
