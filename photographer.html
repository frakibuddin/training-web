Photographer Course

Hypermedia graphic operator

The course is dedicated to the training of professional photographers.

The educational offer of this course is not based only on the acquisition of photography techniques but also on the acquisition of the cultural knowledge behind what is a real art. For this reason, the student will acquire knowledge of the aesthetic, communicative and functional aspects of multimedia products.

The mission is to train professionals capable of offering services to the world of events, entertainment and also cultural and journalistic services.

In addition to the development of the photographic product, the course will also provide the post-production skills of the multimedia document.
